MORE ABOUT THIS CALCULATOR

Our carbon calculator is easy to use and it delivers your total estimated personal carbon emissions with a high degree of accuracy using the best available scientific data.

Like all online carbon calculators, it includes a margin of error due to the complexity of measuring emissions from daily activities such as commuting, housing energy use, and dietary choices.

This calculator focuses on emissions you can influence most directly residential energy, personal transportation, and food-related emissions. It does not include commercial or industrial emissions.
